How the Roundup Works

  • Read each sentence. Every card has a collective noun (like team or jury) with a missing verb.
  • Pick an answer. Tap a verb button on any card, in any order. You can change your mind before submitting.
  • Submit when ready. Hit Submit Answers to lock in every card you've filled in. You only get 3 submit tries for the whole game, so make them count.
  • Each sentence only scores once. The very first time a sentence is submitted, that result is final — +10 seconds if it was right, −10 seconds if it wasn't. You can still change your answer on that card afterward, but it won't add or take away any more time.
  • Results stay a secret. After you submit, you'll only see how many were right and how many were wrong overall — not which specific sentences.
  • Win by correctly answering all 15 before the timer hits zero or you run out of submit tries.
